About us

Wild Coffee beans are more than just coffee- each one represents support provided for a developing community. Our motto is "Every bean counts" - we have found that over time, the little acts of support and generosity really do add up to make wonderful things happen.

As a registered Social Enterprise, we are more determined than ever to continue the work we began some years ago.

Our history

Wild Coffee was founded in 2012 by group of high quality coffee enthusiasts. The company was formed as a means of buying the coffee at a good price. With the amazing support of friends and strangers alike, we established our brand and opened our first caf√©- Wild Coffee.

We continue to buy green coffee directly from farmers. We offer them a fair price- usually determined by matching and raising the price of other buyers. We then bring it back to our coffee place, where it is roasted and sold to our amazing customers.

We're so grateful for our customers. We buy our green coffee bean on faith, trusting in the support of those who believe in our dream to get us through to the next harvest.
